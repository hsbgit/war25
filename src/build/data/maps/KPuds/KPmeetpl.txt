PUD Name:     The Meeting Place
Author:       Cobra
File Name:    KPmeetpl.pud
Description:  Meeting Place by Cobra
Dimensions:   128 x 128
Terrain:      Winter
No Players:   6
 
% Open Land:  39
% Forest:     39
% Water:      17
% Mountains:  5
 
Total Gold:   720,000 (13 mines)
Total Oil:    0
Total Lumber: 535,800
Start Mines:  60,000
Other Mines:  40,000 - 120,000

Default Res:  G=4000  L=3000  O=2000
Default Unit: 1 Peasant, 1 Farm, 1 Town Hall, 1 Guard Tower

Description:  Very good War map. Everyone is in their own little area with the
              only way out is through the middle.  Therfore the Meeting Place.
              Big gold mine in Middle.

Game Setup:   Starting positions are alternating so fixed order, TVB, would
              place allies in every other slot in a 3vs3 game.
