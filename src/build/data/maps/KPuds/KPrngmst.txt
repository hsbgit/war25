Author:       Jason Yeung (jyeung@sfu.ca)

File Name:    KPrngmst.pud
Description:  Ringmaster by Pharkath
Dimensions:   128 x 128
Terrain:      Forest
No Players:   4
 
% Open Land:  34
% Forest:     19
% Water:      47
% Mountains:  0
 
Total Gold:   600,000 (9 mines)
Total Oil:    240,000 (8 patches)
Total Lumber: 243,900
Start Mines:  60,000
Other Mines:  60,000 - 120,000

Default Res:  G=4000  L=3000  O=2000
Default Unit: 1 Peasant, 1 Transport, 1 Farm, 1 Town Hall
 
Description:  A defensive battle for the high seas.  Each player has a private
              lake for their foundary and oil harvesting.  Get established on
              your other island quickly, though.

Game Setup:   Starting positions are opposite so fixed order, TVB, would
              place allies opposite each other.
