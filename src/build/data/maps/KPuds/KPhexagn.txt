PUD Name:     The Hexagon
Author:       Alterac Stormbringer (alterac@monmouth.com)
File Name:    KPhexagn.pud
Description:  Hexagon by Alterac
Dimensions:   128 x 128
Terrain:      Winter
No Players:   6
 
% Open Land:  35
% Forest:     19
% Water:      45
% Mountains:  1
 
Total Gold:   810,000 (9 mines)
Total Oil:    300,000 (14 patches)
Total Lumber: 257,500
Start Mines:  90,000
Other Mines:  90,000

Default Res:  G=2000  L=1500  O=2000
Default Unit: 1 Peasant, 1 Farm, 1 Inventor, 1 Town Hall

Description:  A six sided combination land and sea battle with plenty of 
              resources, protected seaports, and large expansion mines to fight
              over.

Game Setup:   Starting positions are alternating so fixed order, TVB, would
              place allies in every other slot in a 3vs3 game.
