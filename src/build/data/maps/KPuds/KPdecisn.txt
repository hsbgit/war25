Dimensions:   128 x 128
Terrain:      Winter
No Players:   4

% Mountains:  5
 
Total Gold:   580,000 (9 mines)
Total Oil:    120,000 (8 patches)

Start Mines:  80,000

              air - or go for the mine in the middle.  These are the
              decisions you have to make.
 
Game Setup:   Starting positions are adjacent so fixed order, TVB, would
              place allies next to each other.
